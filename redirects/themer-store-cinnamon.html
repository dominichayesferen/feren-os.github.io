Themer Store is no more

On the 26th, I formally announced the closure of Themer Store on the 31st August 2019. On the 30th, I made a blog post explaining why. Now, today, on the 31st, I've kept to my promise. From today, onwards, you can use Store to install themes. Go into Store > Customisation > Themes to install them.
